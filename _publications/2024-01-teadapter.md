We propose TEAdapter, a lightweight plugin for diffusion models that achieves fine-grained controllable music generation by extracting chord, melody, and instrument features from teacher music. We also design a structure-function-based (Intro/Chorus/Outro) multi-adapter collaboration and inpainting mechanism to address structural coherence in long audio generation.
