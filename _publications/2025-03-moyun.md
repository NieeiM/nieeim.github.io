We propose a Chinese calligraphy generation model based on Vision Mamba and TripleLabel mechanism for generating Chinese calligraphy of specified calligraphers, characters, and styles. We construct the Mobao dataset with over 1.9 million Chinese calligraphy images. The Moyun model achieves excellent performance in calligraphy structural fidelity and style matching.
