We propose the Latent Affective Representation Alignment mechanism for continuous fine-grained emotion control in music generation models. The method accepts continuous valence-arousal values as input, effectively decoupling emotional attributes from music content, and significantly outperforms baselines in both emotional accuracy and generation quality.
